Pleotek Competition Winners

PleoTek are happy to announce we are the winners of the Best Idea from the Faculty of Medicine, Health and Life Sciences Award hosted by @insidequbsu.

Congratulations to Ronan at @_microroots for winning the Best Green Business Idea and thank you to @naomi_mcgregor for your ongoing support and congratulations on being a judge at this years What’s The Big Idea? Competition.
