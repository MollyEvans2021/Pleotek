Pleocare Personal

Pleocare Personal is a free to use, web-based care assistant software package designed to assist care workers within the health and social care sectors with shift management, scheduling, billing, and integrated e-training processes.

Shift Management

Pleocare Personal gives carers the ability to manange all aspects of shift booking.

Shift Booking

View a real-time shift feed where agencies/organisations post up to date shifts required in your local area.

Shift Communication

Detailed overview of each booked shift, including contact info, directions, reviews and more.

Shift Confirmation

Confirm a shifts completion through the Pleocare app. Gone are the days of paper based tracking and confirmation.

Shift Requests

Agencies or care homes can request the same carer again, thus building a connection between carer and patient to improve overall quality of care.

Manage your Pleo Profile

  • Pleo Profile

    Pleo Profiles are a care workers digital footprint with the Pleocare ecosystem. Profiles can be viewed by employers, and evolve with you as you take your journey through health and social care.

  • Training and qualifications

    Your Pleo Profile allows you to track and manage your training, qualifications and e-processes in 1 centralised location.

  • Shift History

    Your Pleo account also lets you manage and track your earnings, total hours and work history.
